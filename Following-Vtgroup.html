Followed Virtual YouTubers Groups

hololive ホロライブ

hololive production is a VTuber management group that consists of the talent groups hololive and HOLOSTARS. hololive production has over 80 affiliated talents active in Japan, Indonesia, and English-speaking countries, and has over 80 million YouTuber subscribers all across the channels.

NIJISANJI にじさんじ

NIJISANJI Project, colloquially known as just NIJISANJI, is an agency of primarily 2D Virtual YouTubers who are also known as "Virtual Livers" due to posting on sites outside of YouTube, such as NicoNico, Twitch and Bilibili. Full introduction
